WELCOME TO ROCKS!

Hi, I'm Heidi! I created this website to catalog my rock collection, and allow others to add their finds to my directory!

My collection consists of rocks found around Michigan. Soon, I hope to find more from around the country and eventually around the world!

While most of the specimens featured in the collection are rocks, some are also shells! Michigan's freshwater beaches have lots of beautiful shells to find, along with plenty of rocks.

My collection also features some fossils I collected in southern Michigan from a silica shale deposit. These are Devonian age fossils of a number of different phylums!
